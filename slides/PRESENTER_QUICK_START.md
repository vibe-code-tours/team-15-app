## 🎯 The 3 Key Messages

No matter what, always communicate:

1. **🌍 Problem We Solve**
   > "50 million tons of e-waste yearly. We connect donors with people in need."

2. **💡 How It Works**
   > "List items → People find them → Arrange pickup → Device gets reused"

3. **🎯 Why It Matters**
   > "Real environmental impact. Production-ready platform. Not just a project."

---

## ⏱️ 5-Minute Version (Elevator Pitch)

If you only have 5 minutes, cover:

1. **Slide 1** (15 sec): "This is ReVive - peer-to-peer e-waste platform"
2. **Slide 2** (45 sec): "E-waste is a huge problem. We solve it by connecting donors with people who need devices."
3. **Slide 3** (60 sec): "Our system has 4 parts: the website you see, our computer system behind the scenes, and secure storage."
4. **Slide 12** (60 sec): "Users can donate, browse, request, schedule pickups, message, track impact, and earn badges."
5. **Slide 15** (60 sec): "Complete, secure, scalable platform that makes a real difference. Questions?"

## 🗣️ Jargon Busters (Instant Translations)

| If You Must Say... | Immediately Add... |

| "API" | " - that's the messenger between your screen and our computers" |
| "Database" | " - think of it as a giant organized filing cabinet" |
| "Authentication" | " - like showing your ID at a nightclub" |
| "Frontend" | " - the part you see and interact with" |
| "Backend" | " - the behind-the-scenes computer system" |
| "Responsive" | " - looks great on phones, tablets, and computers" |
| "Scalable" | " - can grow from hundreds to millions of users" |

## ❓ 3 Most Likely Questions

### Q1: "What makes this different from Craigslist/Facebook Marketplace?"
> "ReVive is 100% free donations focused on recycling. We track
> environmental impact, and the whole system is designed specifically
> for keeping electronics out of landfills."

### Q2: "Is it safe to meet strangers?"
> "We recommend public places, daytime, and bringing a friend.
> You can chat through the app first to get comfortable.
> Standard safety practices for any online meetup."

### Q3: "How do you make money?"
> "Right now, we're focused on the environmental mission and
> community impact. Future options could include premium features
> or recycling center partnerships, but core service stays free."
